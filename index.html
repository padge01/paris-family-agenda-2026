Travel basics.

Enough logistics to understand the schedule, without turning the page into an operations sheet.

Monday

  • 05:55 flight from Cork to Paris Beauvais.
  • 08:30 arrival in France.
  • Actual flight time is about 1h35 because France is one hour ahead.
  • A01 Aerobus to Porte Maillot is about 1h30+, traffic-dependent.
  • Taxi recommended from Porte Maillot to the apartment with luggage and stroller.
  • Airbnb check-in is after 15:00.

Tuesday

  • Only full Paris day.
  • Best shape: one morning anchor, one afternoon direction, one good café or pastry stop.
  • Louvre interior is not a good fit here; the Louvre is closed Tuesday.
  • If Monday becomes too tired, Tuesday morning can protect Notre‑Dame.

Wednesday

  • Travel morning, not a sightseeing morning.
  • Early taxi to Porte Maillot.
  • A01 bus around 06:45 for the 10:15 Beauvais flight.
  • 10:15 Paris Beauvais to Cork.
  • Actual flight time is about 1h40.
