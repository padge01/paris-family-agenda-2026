What are these places?

Heart any “must-sees.” Short explanations only — no one needs to study Paris history to vote.

Rue du Bac

Small chapel tied to the Miraculous Medal devotion; one of the most meaningful Catholic stops.

map

Saint‑Nicolas

The traditional Catholic / SSPX anchor in Paris, close to Notre‑Dame and the Latin Quarter.

map

St‑Étienne‑du‑Mont

Beautiful old church near the Panthéon, connected with St. Geneviève, patroness of Paris.

map

Notre‑Dame

The iconic cathedral of Paris. Even an exterior visit gives the “we are in Paris” feeling.

map

Sainte‑Chapelle

A royal medieval chapel famous for walls of stained glass. Probably the prettiest interior.

map

Saint‑Germain

One of Paris’s oldest churches, surrounded by cafés, shops, and pretty streets.

map

Arc de Triomphe

The huge monument at the end of the Champs‑Élysées. Classic Paris photo territory.

map

Eiffel view

Trocadéro is the easy Eiffel Tower photo stop. Better than trying to go up the tower this trip.

map

Probably skip

Chartres, Saint‑Denis, Louvre interior, and Eiffel interior are good in general but too costly in time/friction.

Food ideas by plan

These are practical anchors near where we would already be. Check live hours before committing.

If we choose Saints & Old Paris

  • Breakfast: bakery near the Airbnb before heading out.
  • Lunch/snack: Café Panis or a quick Latin Quarter stop near Notre‑Dame.
  • Dinner: Latin Quarter / Notre‑Dame area, simple French/Italian.

If we choose Easy Paris

  • Breakfast: Mamiche or Du Pain et des Idées near the 10th.
  • Lunch: casual café/brasserie; do not chase a reservation.
  • Dinner: Bouillon Julien or another easy early dinner near base.

If we choose Classic Paris

  • Coffee/pastry: near Notre‑Dame or Tuileries.
  • Lunch: quick sandwich/café so we do not lose the day.
  • Dinner: near Arc/Champs or taxi back toward the Airbnb.

If we choose Cafés & Pretty Streets

  • Coffee: Coutume near Rue du Bac.
  • Pastry: Maison Mulot near Saint‑Germain.
  • Dinner: Saint‑Germain brasserie or flexible central option.
