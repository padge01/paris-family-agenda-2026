Monday afternoon blocks

Arrival day should be meaningful but not punishing. One of these is enough.

M1 · Old Catholic Paris

15:00–19:30 Saint‑Nicolas → St‑Étienne‑du‑Mont → Notre‑Dame exterior → dinner.

best Catholic fitmoderate walkingLatin Quarter dinner
What & why
  • Saint‑Nicolas‑du‑Chardonnet: SSPX/traditional Catholic anchor for the whole trip.
  • St‑Étienne‑du‑Mont: beautiful old church near the Panthéon; St Geneviève connection.
  • Notre‑Dame exterior: iconic, close by, no long museum commitment.
  • Why choose it: most meaningful version of Paris for your family’s interests.
  • Skip if: everyone is wrecked after the airport/bus transfer.
